Expanse Project

What is Expanse?

Expanse is an open source decentralized application platform based on Ethereum, with a greater focus on usability and coexistance within the existing cryptocurrency frameworks

Expanse is driven by a meritocracy of talented individuals, joining together with new ideas and directions, while sharing in the credit and future potential.

Why Expanse? Because Expanse...

  • has a stronger focus on privacy
  • is distributed more fairly (we dont double dip)
  • is way easier to use
  • it just makes sense
